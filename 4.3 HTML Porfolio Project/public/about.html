About me

Current

I am an agile project management consultant in Tokyo, Japan. Currently I am studying web development and AI. I have worked in the project management space for 3 years. I have extensive experience with:

  • Jira - Project task management and Agile development
  • Confluence - Project documentation
  • Trello - Task management for non-techincal teams
  • Asana - Project management
  • M-Files - document and records management and office automation

Prior work

For 5 years I worked as an English and IT teacher in Machida, Kawasaki, and Yokohama. I have a passion for teaching language and helping my students achieve their goals.

For 6 years I worked as a workflow consultant for a large American corporation. I helped companies improve their document and information processes using various technologies such as multifunction printers, high-speed scanners, document management systems, and workflow automation tools. I typically could make general business processes between 60% to 90% faster.

For 6 years I was a university instructor at the University of Alberta, Department of East Asian Studies. In addition to teaching Japanese language, I taught courses in Japanese sociolinguistics, Japanese pop culture, Japanese modern literature, and Asian history and culture. I performed research on Japanese paralinguistic features called aizuchi and developed a method for measuring them quantitatively.
